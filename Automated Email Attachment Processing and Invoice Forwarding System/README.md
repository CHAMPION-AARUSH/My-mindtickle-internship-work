Core Purpose of an Automated Email Invoice Processing System

Email Monitoring: Continuously scans incoming emails for attachments.

Invoice Detection: Identifies invoice documents using predefined rules or content checks.

Automated Routing and labelling : Forwards detected invoices to a designated mailbox or system and labels in original mailbox.

Business Purpose of an Automated Email Invoice Processing System

Process Efficiency: Eliminates manual email sorting, reducing processing time and effort.

Accuracy & Reliability: Prevents missed or misrouted invoices, ensuring timely handling.

Scalability: Handles high email volumes consistently without additional manpower.
